About Send His Word Ministries International

A Word from Our Pastor

Welcome to our church family. We believe that Jesus Christ is the hope of the world, and we are devoted to sharing His love with every person and every home. Through worship, the Word, and prayer, lives are transformed and hope is restored.

We invite you to join us—come as you are—and experience the presence and power of God.

What We Believe

Our faith is rooted in Scripture and centered on Jesus Christ. We hold to historic, orthodox Christian beliefs as taught in the Bible.

  • The Bible is the inspired Word of God
  • One God in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit
  • Salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ
  • The present work and gifts of the Holy Spirit

Our Core Values

Prayer

We seek God’s presence in all we do and intercede for our community.

Word

We teach and live by the Scriptures as our foundation and guide.

Love

We serve with compassion, generosity, and unity in the body of Christ.
